About Us

The colloquial word, ‘humhai', literally translates to 'worriless' and that's the vision humhai.com was born with and strives for.

At humhai.com, our mission is to organize the unorganized home services industry that for decades has been dominated largely by unskilled, unverified handymen leading to non-standardized services and frustrating experiences. With rapid urbanization, digitization and global exposure, standards of living are increasing for the common Indian household leading to demand for better home services through well-qualified and trained professionals.

Indian households fast paced lifestyle look for tech-enabled professional home services brands that can deliver convenient & timely services at their doorstep. Realizing the growing gap between demand for trained professionals and supply of unskilled handymen that was affecting millions of households in urban India, befikr.in was founded in 2016 and since then we have been revolutionising the home services industry with a revolution referred as ‘humhai’ who undergo a rigorous multi-layered verification process and are exposed to a standardized induction program sensitizing and skilling them in the art of public dealing with primary focus on women, senior citizens and children. The world is growing and so are our humhai.

Today, our in-house team of handpicked qualified IT professionals - humhai are the hallmark of quality service by offering basic home services like electrical, plumbing, carpentry, air conditioning, masonry, painting, home appliances repair & service, deep cleaning and partial to complete renovations. The above bouquet of home to office services makes befikr.in the one-stop-shop guaranteeing everyone humhai days and humhai nights.
